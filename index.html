How it works

Thanks for downloading the TKMaxx Shrink App. You'll be able to use your device to answer questions, perform tasks and learn how to help TKMaxx tackle loss. As you do, you'll earn points which can be redeemed against badges and prizes. There is no charge for the App itself but you may incur data charges to download the App if you aren't connected to WiFi.
